TO CLOVE



Oh, dear listen to me for once,
Am I here or lost in a trance?
I know you don’t like the word dear,
But I beg you for once you hear. [1]


You are the reason I smile a lot,
And have to go and cannot abort.
The aura that surrounds me, for once you see,
You’re the reason I am in glee. [2]


Oh, beloved, do you know what you are to me?
Do you know or should I make you see?
I bet you know but it’s unreal to believe,
Don’t worry, I promise I would never leave. [3]


Yes, my sweetness my loved one,
You’ve got it right, you’re the one.
The dear elder sister the Di... I have,
My dear chechi for whom I laugh. [4]


I know you are troubled and busy at work,
And all your hardships have given you circles dark.
Alas! I am not able to make you smile,
And for me it’s a sin very vile. [5]


But for once I wish you to rely on me,
You’re not only my sister, I am the brother of thee.
Oh, Clove for once you believe,
For a smile of yours I can offer mine, happily. [6]


I wish all your pain vanishes away,
But I am well aware that’s not how the Gods play.
Hence, I offer here my smile to them,
Let me take all your sorrows and my smiles you gain. [7]


Ah! Clove, I promise you, I will not to leave,
I wish to be your brother from now till eternity.
Hey dear I know I am not worth more,
Yet I want to stay your closer. [8]


Yes, I intend to be so close to you,
You come to me when sorrows strike you.
Oh, clove my hands are shaking and eyes in tears
I suppose it is the love I carry for yours. [9]


Yes, I am afraid to lose you.
But you need not worry if circumstances make you
I will be here and will never forget
I will greet you again with all I get. [10]


With all my love my care for you
For you are the one I look up to
Just never get upset never you cry
For you have a brother who will make your sorrows pry. [11]


Ahh! How much I wish I once will meet,
The parents of yours and happily greet.
Greet them with respect and a lot of love,
Cause your family is mine for you’re my clove. [12]


If I keep writing these lines won’t end,
Cause I truly love and never pretend.
If I start to tell what you are to me,
All paper of world will cease to be. [13]


My beloved sister before I remain,
Let me introduce you to my parents again.
Yes, you will call them parents too,
For we are a family and you’re a part too. [14]


Let me stop writing here my Clove,
For these are never ending words of love.
I promise one day the time will come,
And remember the days of hardships gone. [15]


How much I wish reincarnation was true
I would wish to have you over again and through. [With love]


From your li'l one
